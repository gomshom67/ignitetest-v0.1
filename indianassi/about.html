About Us

A real-world national ministry raising up a generation for Jesus in the Catholic Church.
Ignite Youth has a bold vision for the future of the Church.
We are dedicated to gathering, raising up and sending out a new
generation of Catholics who are on fire for Jesus, filled with the Holy Spirit,
formed in the faith and centred in the Eucharist.
We believe in real-world evangelisation; revealing Jesus Christ through a ministry
of authentic relationships. Through our national conferences, local events and relational
ministry, we see a generation who are not just changed for a day but transformed for life.
Using contemporary culture to engage their hearts and minds, our dream is to spearhead
a new evangelisation of our nation and pave a way for the Church of the future.
